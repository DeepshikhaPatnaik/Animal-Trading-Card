Cats

Owning a cat can reduce the risk of stroke and heart attack by a third.

  • Scientific Name: Felidae
  • Average Weighing: 4 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Average Height: 25 cm

The domestic cat is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats. Cats are often valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin.
